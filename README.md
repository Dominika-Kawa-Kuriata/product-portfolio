# Product Portfolio — Dominika Kawa-Kuriata

A small collection of product work and side projects. The focus is on how I think and operate as a Product Owner: discovery, decision-making, stakeholder collaboration and increasingly AI-augmented workflows.

## About me

Product Owner with 5+ years in enterprise and SaaS, currently working on internal platforms for a 1000+ employee organisation. Before that, Product Analyst at EY on a global B2B SaaS audit platform. Based in Wrocław, fluent in Polish, English (C1) and French (B2).

Certified in Agentic AI (ServiceNow), Professional Scrum Product Owner II, recently completed the 10x Product Bootcamp at Product Academy.

## Case studies

### [01 — Job Search Co-Pilot](./case-studies/01-job-search-copilot.md)

A custom Claude Project that acts as a structured sparring partner for job search: filtering offers, drafting applications, researching companies, simulating interviews. Built as a system prompt with four operating modes plus a case study bank. Template available for anyone to adapt.

### [02 — Little Milestones](./case-studies/02-little-milestones.md)

A parent companion app prototype built with Figma Make and v0, prompting only, free tiers. Two working prototypes, comparison across design quality plus structural control, content validated against WHO developmental guidelines. Shared publicly on LinkedIn.

### [03 — AI-Augmented Proposal Response](./case-studies/03-ai-augmented-rfp.md)

Coordinating a complex client proposal using AI tools across research, architecture mapping, content drafting plus visual assets. Significant time reduction versus the traditional approach, with quality checkpoints to keep AI outputs as drafts rather than finals.

## How I work with AI

Daily AI stack: Claude and ChatGPT for discovery and documentation, Perplexity for research, v0 plus Figma Make for prototyping, Miro AI for workshop synthesis, Runway for visual storytelling. I treat every AI output as a draft, not a final, and run cross-checks with primary sources plus subject-matter experts.
